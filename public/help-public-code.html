How to get your public code (address)

  1. Open Atomic Wallet.
  2. Click on MORE.
  3. Tap Security.
  4. Tap P key and Back up click Show key.
  5. Check i have read and understood.
  6. Open Key.
  7. Select the coin Bitcoin.
  8. Tap Copy.
  9. Paste it back on the withdrawal page into Click code.

Safe to share. Never share your private key or seed phrase.
